The painting resonates with the legacy of Surrealism, particularly in its dreamlike logic and symbolic ambiguity. An immense white flower unfurls in a radial, fan-like formation, functioning less as a natural object than as an architectural canopy. Its exaggerated symmetry and weightless suspension transform it into a symbolic structure—part shelter, part cosmic mechanism. From its center descends a star-like ornament, delicately aligned with the figure below, establishing a vertical axis that subtly organizes the composition and implies a metaphysical connection between the human and the celestial. The surrounding field of small, coral-toned blossoms punctuates the otherwise serene blue background, introducing rhythm and spatial depth. These repeated floral motifs echo decorative traditions, yet their dispersion across an undefined space resists purely ornamental reading.
