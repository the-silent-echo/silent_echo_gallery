Rendered in a clean, graphic style with smooth surfaces and precise lines, the composition is a masterful interplay of movement and stillness. The woman, seen from behind, is a study in elegant repose. Her back is slightly arched, and her head is turned to the profile, her eyes fixed on the distant horizon. She is dressed in a flowing white dress and a wide-brimmed hat, her form creating a powerful visual anchor amidst the chaotic sprawl of the ocean. Behind her, the waves rise in a series of deep blue undulations, their white foam crests creating a dazzling pattern of light and shadow. They seem to stretch to the very edges of the canvas, creating a sense of infinite expanse. A single, stylized sailboat drifts on the distant water, adding a touch of solitude and mystery to the scene. Surrounding her is a lush tapestry of pink dahlias with warm orange centers. The flowers appear almost monumental, their delicate petals and stalks creating a sense of natural grandeur. They bloom on either side of the figure, framing her face and creating a powerful visual rhythm. A cluster of icy blue leaves grows directly from her hair, veiling her left eye like a natural mask.
