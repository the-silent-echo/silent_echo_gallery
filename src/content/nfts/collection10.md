A monolithic, bone-white mountain—composed of smooth, finger-like spires—floats effortlessly upon a flat, ivory plane, rising against a sky of intricate geometric patterns. At the heart of this singular formation lies a dark, vaulted portal. Deep within its shadows, a singular, brilliant sun radiates outward, its light piercing through a tunnel of rhythmic blue streaks. This "radiant core" suggests a source of infinite energy or an ancient, cosmic wisdom hidden within the silent stone. It is the visual personification of an epiphany—a bright truth found in the center of a quiet, unyielding monolith. The sky is not a void, but a meticulously woven tapestry. A grid of blue diamond-shaped "petals" is held together by a golden, lattice-like structure, creating a sense of a manufactured or celestial dome. Floating across this mathematical firmament are strings of spherical, cloud-like orbs, resembling a trail of white pearls or exhaled breaths frozen in time.
